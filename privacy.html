Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025 · AR Akron Services · arakrons@gmail.com

1. Information We Collect

When you purchase FinanceOS, we collect: your name, email address, and payment information (processed securely by Lemon Squeezy). During license activation, we collect a device identifier to bind your license to your device.

We do NOT collect any of your business financial data. All revenue, expense, supplier and payroll data entered in FinanceOS is stored locally on your device only.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to: (a) process your purchase and deliver your license key; (b) send you important updates about your license; (c) provide customer support; (d) prevent license fraud and unauthorized sharing.

3. Data Storage

Your business data entered in FinanceOS is stored exclusively in your browser's local storage on your own device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Your purchase information (name, email, license key) is stored securely in our database hosted on Supabase, with industry-standard encryption.

4. Third-Party Services

Lemon Squeezy — our payment processor. They handle all payment data. See their privacy policy at lemonsqueezy.com/privacy.

Supabase — our database provider for license management. Data is stored in EU servers.

Google Analytics — we use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website and app. This service collects: your IP address (anonymized), browser type, pages visited, time spent on pages, and general location (city/country level). You can opt out of Google Analytics by using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on. No personally identifiable information is collected through Analytics.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except as necessary to: process your payment (Lemon Squeezy), provide the service (Supabase), or analyze website usage (Google Analytics).

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EU/EEA, you have the right to: access your personal data, correct inaccurate data, request deletion of your data, object to processing, and withdraw consent for analytics tracking. Contact us at arakrons@gmail.com to exercise these rights.

7. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies for: (a) essential operation (session management, license validation); (b) analytics (Google Analytics — requires your consent via the cookie banner). You can manage your cookie preferences at any time using the banner at the bottom of the page or by adjusting your browser settings.

8. Data Retention

We retain your purchase and license information for as long as your license is active and for up to 3 years after for legal and accounting purposes.

9. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at arakrons@gmail.com.
